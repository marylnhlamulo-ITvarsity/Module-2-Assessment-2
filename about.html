ABOUT

Pine City Mall is a 67 000m2 regional shopping centre which rests on the southern banks of the Pine Lake in Pine City. The mall features a unique 160-metre-long steam boat that creates an aesthetically beautiful shopping complex.With over 130 stores and restaurants, Pine City Mall offers the community convenience, product variety and great value.With stores such as Shoprite, Woolworths, Pick n Pay, Dischem, Game, Clicks and Mr Price and services including a full variety of banking options, the Post office and E-toll, Pine City Mall truly is your community convenience shopping destination..
